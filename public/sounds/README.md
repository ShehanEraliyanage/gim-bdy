# Sound Files

Add your birthday celebration sound files here:

- **birthday-music.mp3** - Background birthday music (will loop during photo display)
- **transition.mp3** - Sound effect for transitioning between photos  
- **unwrap.mp3** - Sound effect for unwrapping the gift

You can find royalty-free audio at:
- https://pixabay.com/music/
- https://freepik.com/
- https://www.soundly.com/

Or use online converters to convert your files to MP3 format.
